Our Vision and Mission

Our Vision

  • To be a global leader in chemical and biosensor innovations, ensuring safety and quality in food and healthcare through accessible and advanced technologies.

    • Our Mission

  • Lead the development of cutting-edge chemical sensors and diagnostic systems to enhance food safety and healthcare standards.

  • Deliver affordable, user-friendly diagnostic and quality assurance tools, bridging gaps in underserved and resource-limited communities.

  • Commit to designing sustainable, high-quality technologies while fostering collaborations for a safer, healthier, and more equitable world.

    • About Us

    Qualattice Chemitronics India Private Limited, incubated by Indium Laboratory, is a cutting-edge startup dedicated to advancing food safety, agriculture, and healthcare through innovative chemical and biosensor technologies. With a vision to be a global leader in sensor innovation, we design sustainable, user-friendly, and affordable diagnostic solutions tailored to underserved and resource-limited communities. Our expertise in nano-sensor fabrication ensures precise and reliable tools that bridge critical gaps in quality assurance and health monitoring. Backed by the scientific excellence of Indium Laboratory, we are committed to creating a safer, healthier, and more equitable world.
